ALAI UP iCAB/RPV Protocol Toolkit

October 21, 2024
Login to Bookmark
Download this resource

The ALAI UP Protocol Toolkit is an interactive planning tool for administrators, clinicians, and staff at clinical sites who are interested in or are in the process of developing an injectable cabotegravir/rilpivirine (iCAB/RPV) program. The toolkit was developed by ALAI UP, a Special Projects of National Significance (SPNS) and Minority HIV/AIDS Fund project to help clinics across the United States develop injectable HIV treatment programs with the goal of addressing inequity in health outcomes.

Toolkit Components

The Toolkit breaks down the complex process of long-acting injectable ART (LAI ART) implementation into manageable and coherent program components. It guides users through key decisions and health equity considerations in clinical protocol development. Access the ALAI UP Protocol Toolkit Instructions and the editable Word versions of the ALAI UP Protocol Template and the five protocol Overviews and Worksheets on this page. You can also access the PDF version of the iCAB/RPV Protocol Toolkit.

Engaging Patients Overview & Worksheet

The “Engaging Patients Protocol Overview” is a planning document for administrators, clinicians, and staff at clinical sites who are interested in, or are in the process of, developing an injectable cabotegravir/rilpivirine (iCAB/RPV) program. It can be used in conjunction with the “Engaging Patients Protocol Worksheet” to facilitate discussion and documentation of key decision points and action items that are crucial in building an equitable iCAB/RPV treatment protocol. Answers from the Worksheet can be plugged into their corresponding placeholder in the “Protocol Template.”
