Virtual Game Guide

June 1, 2021
Login to Bookmark
Download this resource

Interactive Exercises for Trainers to Teach Quality Improvement in HIV Care Online

The CQII Virtual Game Guide was designed to make over 25 games and exercises more broadly available to teach quality improvement more effectively in virtual learning settings. Trainers, teachers, and educators can utilize this Virtual Game Guide to interactively engage anyone else who is interested in improving HIV care. The use of these virtual games can make your online meetings livelier and most importantly, teach valuable quality improvement skills.

All games described in this Guide have been categorized using six domains to allow you to quickly identify the interactive exercises you need. Included with each game is a brief descriptor, a downloadable copy of the game’s instructions, the associated facilitator slide set, and any other handout.

Ice Breakers
  • Four Corners. An icebreaker that allows participants to get to know each other and to find a group identity. Four Corners PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Quotations. A knowledge-testing game that requires collaboration among participants. Slides (PPTX). Quotations PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Parade of Flags. An icebreaker that allows participants to get to know each other and to find a group identity. Parade of Flags PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • No “I” in Team. A game to get to know each other and strengthen communication skills. No I in Team PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Knot or Not. An interactive game to guess the result based on demonstrations. Knot or Not PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
Team Building and Cooperation
  • Crossing the Line First. An icebreaker game to get to know each other and learn about win-win situations. Crossing the Line First PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Survival Games. A team problem-solving game that emphasizes prioritization and collaboration.
    • Survive the Moon Handout Survive the Moon Handout (DOC) - Survive the Moon PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
    • Survive the Sea Survive at Sea Handout (DOC) - Survive at Sea PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Zin Obelisk. A competitive game among teams to teach problem-solving and group decision-making. Zin Obelisk Participant Handout (DOC) - Zin Obelisk Facilitator Handout (DOC) - Zin Obelisk PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • What Should We Do About the Neighbors?. An icebreaker that fosters problem-solving and strengthens communication skills. What Should We Do About the Neighbors PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • How High Will You Bid?. An interactive icebreaker that teaches about taking risks. Slides (PPTX)
  • Headliner. An exercise to outline key successes and accomplishments of a team. Headliner PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
Quality Improvement Capacity
  • Poll Everywhere Competition. A timed multiple-choice quiz where everyone participates individually. Poll Everywhere Handout (DOCX)
  • QI Jeopardy. An interactive question-and-answer game to learn about key concepts. QI Jeopardy General HIV PowerPoint Slides (PPT) - QI Jeopardy Social Determinants of Health PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Who Wants to Be A Millionaire. A multiple-choice knowledge-testing game that requires collaboration. Who Wants to be a Millionaire PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Scavenger Hunt. A team-based game that emphasizes interaction and teamwork. Scavenger Hunt PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
Data and Measurement
  • Draw the Pig. An interactive drawing game that teaches standardization. Draw the Pig PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Win As Much as You Can. An interactive team-based game to competitively learn about making group decisions. Win as Much as You Can Handout (DOC) Win as Much a You Can PowerPoint (PPTX)
  • Public Goods. A competitive game of teams to learn about balancing diverse interests. Public Goods PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Estimation. A guessing game to learn about the increased accuracy of teams compared to individuals. Estimation Handout (DOC) Estimation Handout PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
Testing and Making Changes
  • Mr. Potato Head. A team building game to identify key essential elements and to understand team roles. Mr. Potato Head PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Cash Register. A problem-solving game that requires collaboration and sound listening skills. Cash Register Handout (DOC) Cash Register PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • NY Times Problem-Solving Puzzle. A problem-solving game that requires identifying patterns in numbers to test individual hypotheses. NY Times Problem Solving Puzzle PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Selling Spread. An interactive demonstration about spreading new innovations and how others adopt new ideas. Selling Spread PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
Systems Thinking
  • Candle Problem. A problem-solving game that requires individual creativity. Candle Problem PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Peanut Butter and Jelly. A demonstration with everyone participating that teaches proper documentation of process steps. Peanut Butter and Jelly PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)
  • Think Inside the Box. A team-based game to test different problem-solving skills. Think Inside the Box PowerPoint Slides (PPTX)

Developed by

Center for Quality Improvement and Innovation (CQII)
New York State Department of Health AIDS Institute
