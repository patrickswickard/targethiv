Top Picks for Ryan White Community Members

The Ryan White Community is comprised of the people and programs that provide and receive HIV/AIDS care under the HRSA Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program (RWHAP). These pages highlight key links and resources for some of the groups in the Ryan White Community.

  • Administrators, Directors, and Managers - oversee RWHAP grants and ensure compliance with legislative and program requirements.
  • Case Managers - including social workers, nurse case managers, and others.
  • Clinicians - doctors, nurses, physicians assistants, pharmacists, dentists, and everyone involved in care delivery.
  • Data Managers - manage the client level data systems (RSR, ADR) and work with CAREWare.
  • HRSA HIV/AIDS Bureau - project officers, planners, and administrators.
  • Oral Health Professionals - dentists, dental hygienists, and dental assistants.
  • People with HIV and Community - patients, family, and community members.
  • Planning Councils and Planning Bodies - planning administrative staff and planning body members.
  • Quality Leads - staff responsible for developing and implementing clinical quality management programs.
