2027-2031 Integrated HIV Prevention and Care Plan Development Toolkit

The materials, tools, and resources included below are intended to support development of Integrated HIV Prevention and Care Plans. These lists are not static and will be added to over time.

Have questions about the new Guidance? See our FAQs.

Don't know what you need? Contact IHAP. 

HRSA and CDC Guidance
  • Integrated HIV Prevention and Care Plan Guidance, including the Statewide Coordinated Statement of Need, CY 2027-2031. The guidance outlines the planning requirements for RWHAP Parts A and B recipients and all DHP-funded state and local health departments. Submission of the Integrated HIV Prevention and Care Plan meets HRSA and CDC legislative and program requirements, and serves as a jurisdictional HIV strategy guiding all HIV- related resources for the jurisdiction.
Tools and Resources
  • Simplified Checklist to Support 2027-2031 Integrated HIV Prevention and Care Plan Submission. A checklist is required in the submission of the Integrated Plans for 2027-2031. Use this simplified, fillable checklist to indicate which elements required new content and which elements were developed as part of another jurisdictional plan.
  • Summary of the 2022-2026 Integrated HIV Prevention and Care Plans. This document highlights findings from the IHAP TAC's review of the 2022-2026 Integrated HIV Prevention and Care Plans, including innovative practices that can support jurisdictions to effectively develop, implement, monitor, evaluate, and improve their Integrated Plans.
  • Integrated Plan Work Plan and Monitoring Tool. This template provides jurisdictions with a dynamic tracking tool to centralize and operationalize their Integrated Plan and is intended to be used as a "living document" to reflect the most up-to-date progress.  
  • HIV Prevention, Care and Treatment Resource Inventory Compiler. This resource supports the collection and analysis of information about HIV funding in the jurisdiction. The Excel tool generates a formatted table for inclusion in the Integrated Plan submission and a dashboard for data analysis.
  • Integrated Planning Video Series. These brief videos offer an introduction to and foundational information about integrated planning, Integrated HIV Prevention and Care Plans, and the Integrated HIV Prevention and Care Guidance.
