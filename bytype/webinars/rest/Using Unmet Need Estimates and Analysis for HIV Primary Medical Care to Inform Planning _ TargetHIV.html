Using Unmet Need Estimates and Analysis for HIV Primary Medical Care to Inform Planning

April 19, 2021
Login to Bookmark
Download this resource

This document is designed to help RWHAP recipients understand how Unmet Need estimates and analyses fit into larger planning processes. It gives specific examples of how Unmet Need estimates and analyses can support planning for:

  • Priority Setting and Resource Allocation (PRSA)

  • Integrated/Comprehensive Plans

  • Needs Assessment

  • Planning for National Initiatives like the Ending the HIV Epidemic Initiative (EHE)

  • Special Analyses

Developed by

Abt Associates
HRSA HIV/AIDS Bureau (HAB)
