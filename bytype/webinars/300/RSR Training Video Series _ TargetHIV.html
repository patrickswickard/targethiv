RSR Training Video Series

December 22, 2023
Login to Bookmark

This series of videos covers the fundamental concepts behind the Ryan White Services Report (RSR) for new and experienced grantees.

Components

  • Data Quality for the RSR
    December 21, 2023
    This 10-minute video is designed to support RWHAP recipients and providers in improving their data quality.
  • Software Options for the RSR
    December 21, 2023
    This video is designed to support RWHAP recipients and providers in exploring software options to create their RSR Client-Level Data XML file.
  • Video: Introduction to the RSR
    September 7, 2022
    Introductory video on the Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program (RWHAP) Services Report (RSR).

Developed by

Data Integration, Systems and Quality (DISQ) Team
