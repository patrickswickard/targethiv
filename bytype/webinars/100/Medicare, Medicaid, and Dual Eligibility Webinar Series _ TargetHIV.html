Medicare, Medicaid, and Dual Eligibility Webinar Series

Webinar series covering Medicare, Medicaid, and Medicare-Medicaid dual eligibility for Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program (RWHAP) clients. This webinar series was tailored for program managers, case managers and other staff that conduct outreach, benefits counseling and enrollment activities, as well as the recipient staff that support these programs.

We thank those of you who joined us for one or more sessions and helped contribute to the success of this series. We’re excited to have expanded our webinar offerings into Medicaid and dual eligibility for RWHAP clients. Keep an eye out for future opportunities! Webinar materials from each of the four presentations are available for review under the corresponding section header on this page.

This webinar covered eligibility criteria for Medicare and how people can enroll, the different Medicare parts, and the roles of the RWHAP in supporting clients with Medicare costs.

Access the webinar materials

This webinar covered: external Medicare enrollment supports, how to identify and manage common Medicare enrollment challenges, and sources of financial assistance for Medicare costs.

Access the webinar materials

This webinar covered: an overview of the Medicaid program, eligibility and enrollment rules and covered services, the difference between Medicaid managed care and fee-for-service Medicaid, how Medicaid coverage may differ from state to state, how RWHAP recipients can find out what is available in their states, and how the RWHAP supports clients enrolled in Medicaid.

Access the webinar materials

This webinar covered: health coverage considerations for RWHAP clients who are dually eligible, best practices for helping dually eligible clients enroll in health coverage, financial assistance options, sources of enrollment support for RWHAP case managers and program administrators.
