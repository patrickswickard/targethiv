What Works in HIV Care & Services Podcast

The What Works in HIV Care & Services Podcast highlights best practices featured in the RWHAP Best Practices Compilation and provides tips for replicating approaches in your settings and communities. 

Listen in to hear each guest speaker share insightful information on how they implemented their approach.

Episode 0 - Introduction

This episode provides an introduction to the multiple programs (called Parts) that make up the Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program (RWHAP), the RWHAP Best Practices Compilation, and the What Works in HIV Care & Services Podcast. We encourage you to check out the Best Practices Compilation to learn more.

Read Episode Transcript

Episode 1 - Housing First to Treat and Prevent HIV

Episode 1 focuses on Housing First to Treat and Prevent HIV, a program implemented by Caracole, a non-profit in Cincinnati, OH.

Read Episode Transcript

Episode 2 - Golden Compass

Episode 2 focuses on Golden Compass, a program implemented by The Ward 86 outpatient HIV clinic at San Francisco General Hospital. Golden Compass aims to serve the unique needs of older adults with HIV.

Read Episode Transcript

Episode 3 - Rapid stART

Episode 3 focuses on Southern Nevada Rapid stART, an initiative led by Clark County Social Service, Office of HIV.

Read Episode Transcript

Episode 4 - Text Me, Girl!

Episode 4 focuses on Text Me, Girl!, an intervention developed and implemented by the Friends Research Institute in Los Angeles, CA.
