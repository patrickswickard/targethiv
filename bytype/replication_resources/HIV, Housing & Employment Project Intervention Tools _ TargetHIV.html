December 5, 2024
Login to Bookmark

Intervention manuals, summaries, and other intervention resources from the HIV, Housing & Employment Project demonstration sites.

AIDS Foundation of Chicago: Safe & Sound Return Partnership

The Safe & Sound Return Partnership (SSRP) is a three-pronged intervention that works at the client, provider, and system levels to rapidly connect PLWH recently released from Cook County Jail and Illinois state prisons to medical, housing, and employment services using a Peer Community Re-engagement Specialist model.

AIDS Foundation of Chicago (AFC), Chicago, IL

AFH/Avenue 360 Project CORE: Coordination of Resources and Employment Services

Through Project CORE (Coordination of Resources and Employment Services) Avenue 360 Health & Wellness offers a medical home for patients and AIDS Foundation Houston coordinates employment training and resources. These agencies believe that coordination is the key to breaking down barriers to health care in their community.

Avenue 360 Health & Wellness, and AIDS Foundation Houston, Houston, TX

Bexar County: The CASE Management Initiative

The CASE Management Initiative (CAre, houSing, and Employment) comprises coordination of client services for six months and transitioning clients to the jurisdiction's case management system after completion of the program. Acuity scores determine clients’ case management paths.

Bexar County Hospital District, San Antonio, TX

City of Pasadena: Operation Link

Short-term intensive case management services to link clients to housing, employment, and health care services provided by a team comprised of a Peer Care Navigator, Housing Navigator, and an Employment Navigator.

City of Pasadena, Pasadena, CA

City of Paterson: Coordination of Supportive Employment and Housing Services

Smart Care Management data-driven/IT model (e.g., patient portal, real-time dashboard for agencies) to enhance utilization of existing health, housing, and employment services.

Family Health Centers of San Diego: Coordinated Services Integration Intervention

Fenway Health: Housing & Employment Project

Partnership between employment services and Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program providers to foster case coordination among medical care and social service providers, including a screening and referral system for housing and employment needs and services individualized for specific patient needs. 

Fenway Health, Boston, MA

GMHC: Project HEALTH

Project HEALTH (Housing & Employment Access Lead to Health) uses care coordination to link clients to services by assessing client needs, barriers, and stages of change, guided by electronic health records and a modified stages of change tool used to assess housing & employment stages of change.

GMHC, New York, NY

Positive Impact Health Centers: H.O.M.E.S.

H.O.M.E.S. (Housing Opportunities, Medical, & Employment Services) is a housing-first model to help people with HIV address their housing instability while working towards obtaining employment and maintaining consistent medical care. Every patient completes an individual service plan, developed in partnership with their case manager.

Positive Impact Health Centers, Atlanta, GA

PRC: Integrative Health Analysis

Clients are enrolled into a low barrier program that provides immediate and critical housing crisis intervention, including in-housing employment services and ongoing intervention services to secure permanent housing and maintain access to HIV primary care.

Yale, Liberty Community Services: Project HERO

Project HERO (Housing and Employment Resources for Improving HIV Outcomes) provides housing and employment services including a Job Club, to provide a supportive environment and training for participants.
